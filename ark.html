經文 · Scripture

出埃及記 25:10–22

中文 · 和合本／當代

10「要用皂莢木做一個櫃，長二肘半，寬一肘半，高一肘半。 11要裡外包上純金，四圍鑲金牙邊。 12也要鑄四個金環，安在櫃的四腳上；這邊兩環，那邊兩環。 13要用皂莢木做兩根槓，用金包裹。 14把槓穿在櫃旁的環內，以便抬櫃。 15這槓要常在櫃的環內，不可抽出來。 16必將我所要賜給你的法版放在櫃裡。

17要用純金做施恩座，長二肘半，寬一肘半。 18要用金子錘出兩個基路伯來，安在施恩座的兩頭。 22我要在那裡與你相會，又要從法櫃施恩座上二基路伯中間，和你說我所要吩咐你傳給以色列人的一切事。」

English · NIV

10"Have them make an ark of acacia wood—two and a half cubits long, a cubit and a half wide, and a cubit and a half high. 11Overlay it with pure gold, both inside and out, and make a gold molding around it. 12Cast four gold rings for it and fasten them to its four feet, with two rings on one side and two on the other. 13Then make poles of acacia wood and overlay them with gold. 16Then put in the ark the tablets of the covenant law, which I will give you.

17Make an atonement cover of pure gold—two and a half cubits long and a cubit and a half wide. 18And make two cherubim out of hammered gold at the ends of the cover. 22There, above the cover between the two cherubim, I will meet with you and give you all my commands for the Israelites."

屬靈意涵 · Reflection

Meditation

同在的中心

約櫃是會幕最中心的器具，其上的施恩座是上主於地上的「寶座」 —— 祂選擇住在兩基路伯展翅之間，住在祂百姓中央。 皂莢木 + 純金的組合，預表將要來的彌賽亞 —— 完全的人性與完全的神性聯合於一身。 而那遮掩律法的施恩座，正預告了上主以血所立的恩典之約。

The Centre of the Presence

The Ark is the innermost piece of the sanctuary, its Mercy Seat the earthly throne of the Lord. Wood and gold prefigure the Messiah — full humanity, full deity, indivisibly one. The cover laid over the tablets foreshadows the new covenant: mercy covering the demands of the Law, blood sprinkled where God and sinner meet.
