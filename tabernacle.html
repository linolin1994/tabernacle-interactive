經文 · Scripture

出埃及記 26 (節選)

中文 · 和合本／當代

1你要用十幅幔子做帳幕；這些幔子要用撚的細麻和藍色、紫色、朱紅色線製造，並用巧匠的手工繡上基路伯。 7你要用山羊毛織十一幅幔子，作為帳幕以上的罩棚。 14又要用染紅的公羊皮做罩棚的蓋；再用海狗皮做一層罩棚上的頂蓋。 15你要用皂莢木做帳幕的豎板。 33要使幔子垂在鉤子下，把法櫃抬進幔子內；這幔子要將聖所和至聖所隔開。

English · NIV

1"Make the tabernacle with ten curtains of finely twisted linen and blue, purple and scarlet yarn, with cherubim woven into them. 7Make curtains of goat hair for the tent over the tabernacle—eleven altogether. 14Make for the tent a covering of ram skins dyed red, and over that a covering of the other durable leather. 15Make upright frames of acacia wood for the tabernacle. 33Hang the curtain from the clasps and place the ark behind the curtain. The curtain will separate the Holy Place from the Most Holy Place."

屬靈意涵 · Reflection

Meditation

裂開的幔子

幔子把人擋在至聖所之外 —— 罪人不能近上主而存活。 一年一次，大祭司帶血進到幔內。但主耶穌斷氣時， 這幔子從上到下裂為兩半。從那一刻起，進入上主同在的路向眾人開通了 —— 這是恩典的時刻。

The Veil Torn

The veil kept sinners out of the Most Holy Place — none could draw near and live. Once a year the high priest entered with blood. But when Christ breathed His last, the veil tore from top to bottom. From that moment the way into the Presence was opened to all — the great moment of grace.
