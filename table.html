中文 · 和合本／當代

23要用皂莢木做一張桌子，長二肘，寬一肘，高一肘半。 24要包上純金，四圍鑲上金牙邊。 25桌子的四圍各做一掌寬的橫梁，橫梁上鑲著金牙邊。 26要做四個金環，安在桌子的四角上，就是桌子四腳上的四角。 29要做桌子上的盤子、調羹，並奠酒的爵和瓶；這都要用精金製作。 30又要在桌子上，在我面前，常擺陳設餅。

English · NIV

23"Make a table of acacia wood—two cubits long, a cubit wide and a cubit and a half high. 24Overlay it with pure gold and make a gold molding around it. 25Also make around it a rim a handbreadth wide and put a gold molding on the rim. 26Make four gold rings for the table and fasten them to the four corners. 29Make its plates and dishes of pure gold, as well as its pitchers and bowls for the pouring out of offerings. 30Put the bread of the Presence on this table to be before me at all times."

屬靈意涵 · Reflection

Meditation

常擺面前的餅

「陳設餅」希伯來原文是「面前的餅」(lechem ha-panim)。 十二個餅代表以色列十二支派 —— 整個族群被擺在上主面前， 被祂看顧、被祂供養、被祂悅納。日後耶穌稱自己是「從天降下的生命之糧」， 這張桌子最終指向祂自己。

Bread Before His Face

The Hebrew is 'lechem ha-panim' — bread of the face, the presence. Twelve loaves for twelve tribes: the whole people held before His gaze, seen, fed, accepted. Centuries later Jesus would say, "I am the bread of life" — and this table finds its deepest fulfilment in Him.
