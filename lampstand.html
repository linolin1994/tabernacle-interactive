經文 · Scripture

出埃及記 25:31–40

中文 · 和合本／當代

31要用純金做一個燈臺；燈臺的座、幹、盞、球、花，都要與燈臺接連一塊，用金子錘出來。 32燈臺兩旁要伸出六個枝子：這旁三個，那旁三個。 33這旁每枝上有三個杯，形狀像杏花，有球，有花；那旁每枝上也有三個，全臺六個枝子都是這樣。 36球和枝子要與燈臺接連一塊，都是一塊精金錘出來的。 37要做燈臺的七個燈盞。祭司要點這燈，使燈光對照。 39做燈臺和這一切的器具要用精金一他連得。

English · NIV

31"Make a lampstand of pure gold. Hammer out its base and shaft, and make its flowerlike cups, buds and blossoms of one piece with them. 32Six branches are to extend from the sides of the lampstand—three on one side and three on the other. 33Three cups shaped like almond flowers with buds and blossoms are to be on one branch, three on the next, and the same for all six branches. 36The buds and branches shall all be of one piece with the lampstand, hammered out of pure gold. 37Then make its seven lamps and set them up on it so that they light the space in front of it. 39A talent of pure gold is to be used for the lampstand and all these accessories."

屬靈意涵 · Reflection

Meditation

聖所裡的光

聖所裡沒有窗 —— 唯一的光源就是這座金燈臺。 它放在桌子對面（南側），照亮陳設餅與整個聖所。 一塊整金錘出，意味著光的來源不可分割； 七盞燈，是上主完全的光，預表「世上的光」與「七靈」。

Light in the Holy Place

The Holy Place had no windows; the lampstand was its only light. Set opposite the table, it lit both the Bread and the room. Hammered from one mass of gold — light's source is one and indivisible; seven flames — completeness, the sevenfold Spirit, and the Light of the world to come.
