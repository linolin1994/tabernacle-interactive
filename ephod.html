中文 · 和合本／當代

6他們要拿金線和藍色、紫色、朱紅色線，並撚的細麻，用巧匠的手工做以弗得。 7以弗得當有兩條肩帶，接上兩頭，使它相連。 8其上巧工織的帶子，要和以弗得一樣的做法。 9要取兩塊紅瑪瑙，在上面刻以色列兒子的名字。 12要將這兩塊寶石安在以弗得的兩條肩帶上，為以色列人作紀念石。亞倫要在兩肩上擔他們的名字，在耶和華面前作為紀念。 14又拿精金，用擰工彷彿擰繩子，做兩條鏈子。

English · NIV

6"Make the ephod of gold, and of blue, purple and scarlet yarn, and of finely twisted linen—the work of skilled hands. 7It is to have two shoulder pieces attached to two of its corners. 8Its skillfully woven waistband is to be like it—of one piece with the ephod. 9Take two onyx stones and engrave on them the names of the sons of Israel. 12Fasten them on the shoulder pieces of the ephod as memorial stones. Aaron is to bear the names on his shoulders as a memorial before the Lord. 14Make two braided chains of pure gold, like a rope."

屬靈意涵 · Reflection

Meditation

擔在肩上的名字

「亞倫要在兩肩上擔他們的名字。」(出 28:12) 肩，是擔負與權柄之處。 十二支派的名字被刻在紅瑪瑙上 —— 不是寫上去的，是刻進去的，永不磨滅。 這預表那位「政權必擔在他的肩頭上」的大祭司， 祂把祂百姓的名字永遠擔在自己身上。

Names Borne on Shoulders

"Aaron is to bear the names on his shoulders as a memorial." (Ex 28:12) Shoulders are the place of burden and of authority. The tribes' names were not written but engraved — never to be erased. Foreshadowing the greater High Priest whose "government shall be upon His shoulder" (Isa 9:6).
